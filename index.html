About Us

Welcome to our Dandiya Nights celebration! Join us for a vibrant series of events that span the joyous festival of Navratri. Our gatherings are dedicated to the spirited dance of Garba and Dandiya Raas, where participants come together to celebrate under the stars, dressed in colorful traditional attire.

Our event page is your go-to resource for all the details you need to join the festivities. Here, you'll find the complete event schedule, information on ticket bookings, and guidelines to ensure you have the best experience possible. From live music performances to beautifully decorated venues, everything is designed to immerse you in the Navratri spirit.

Stay connected with the latest updates, browse through our photo galleries, and take part in exciting contests that foster community spirit. Join us for unforgettable nights filled with dance, music, and celebration as we create lasting memories together!
